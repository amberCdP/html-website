Flatbread

Unlike most breads, flatbread is, as the name suggests, flat. This is because most of the time it is baked in a pan.
Flatbread is soft, chewy and perfect to dip in stews or roll up with different fillings.
There are a lot of different kinds of flatbreads, used by different kinds of cultures; Indian naan, Mexican tortilla's,
Arabic Saj bread, ... The thing they all have in common though, is that they are all delicious!
