French toast

Uhm.. Is this still considered a bread? Well.. I guess it's made out of bread...
Almost every culture has it's own version of french toast. In French it is called "pain perdu" and in Dutch "verloren brood".
When translated, both mean the same thing: lost bread. This name makes sense when you know french toast is made with stale bread.
The bread is then soaked in eggs, milk or cream and then pan-fried. It is mainly served as a sweet dish,
but there are some weirdo's that like it savory, I guess.
You can also make this with stale croissants,
but never let a French person know you did this, as this might lead them to start another revolution.
